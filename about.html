About Us

Derived from a play on the Hiligaynon word “ragomo,” meaning to crunch, be crisp, or emit a crunching sound, Ragoomu opened its doors to the public on October 14, 2022. Our aim is to capture the Ilonggo taste through Western and Asian-inspired crispy chicken dishes.

Conceptualized by high school friends Ely Vasig, Jesse Lagon, and Marc Guillergan, along with Val Vasig and Hannah Vasig-Tan Yau, Ragoomu was created to be a place for quality bonding, joy, and love among families and friends.
